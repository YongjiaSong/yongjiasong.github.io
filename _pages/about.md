I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the [Department of Language Science](https://www.langsci.uci.edu/) at UC Irvine, working with [Dr. Gregory Scontras](https://www.langsci.uci.edu/scontras/). I'm interested in how people process and produce languages, in particular bilinguals/multilinguals. My resarch focuses on bilingual lexical production, lexical entrainment/alignment, and factors related to language production, such as proficiency, language environments, contextual factors, and production modalities. Besides that, I also have some experience on studying scope ambiguity to understand the reasoning process across different contexts. I apply both behavioral and computational methods to investigate the mechanisms involved in language processing.
